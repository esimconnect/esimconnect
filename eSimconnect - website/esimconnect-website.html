3.1 Activation

eSIM plans are activated when your device first connects to the destination network. Day 1 of your plan begins upon activation. Plans with Late Arrival Protection will extend Day 1 if activation occurs between 22:00 and 23:59 local destination time.

eSIM plans require an eSIM-compatible device. It is your responsibility to verify compatibility before purchase. A list of compatible devices is available on our website.

All eSIM purchases are final. No refunds will be issued once an eSIM QR code has been delivered to your email, as the eSIM has been provisioned at that point. In cases of technical failure on our part, we will provide a replacement eSIM or credit.

Last updated: 8 March 2026 · AAAI Ventures, Singapore · PDPA Compliant
eSIM Connect is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA). This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

  • Mobile number — for account verification and OTP forwarding services

  • To process your eSIM order and deliver your QR code

We comply with Singapore's Do Not Call Registry. We will not send unsolicited marketing messages to telephone numbers registered on the DNC Registry. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

4. Data Sharing

We share your data only with the following third parties, strictly as necessary to provide our services:

  • Airalo — to provision eSIM plans (email, order details)
  • Twilio — to provision virtual numbers (mobile number)
  • Stripe — to process payments (email, payment details)
  • SendGrid — to deliver transactional emails (email)
  • Anthropic — AI Travel Companion queries (destination and travel dates only — no personal identifiers)

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as required by law. Order records are retained for 7 years for accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time.
