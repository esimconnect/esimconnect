The Problem

Overseas but still
tied to home.

Every traveller faces the same three problems. Most apps solve one. We solve all three.

📶
Expensive roaming data

Your home carrier charges SGD $10–30/day for roaming. A two-week trip can add hundreds to your bill before you even check the exchange rate.

📵
Missed calls from home

Your bank, tax office, doctor or family tries to call. They reach voicemail. WhatsApp and Telegram don't work for government agencies or hospitals.

🔐
OTP SMS never arrives

Banking apps, Singpass, government portals — all locked behind a one-time SMS password sent to your home SIM. No SMS = locked out completely.
